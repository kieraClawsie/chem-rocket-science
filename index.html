Rocket Science

Space: often referred to as the final frontier, space is vast, dangerous, and almost entirely unexplored. Throughout the past century, humanity has made great strides in space exploration, and at the heart of it all lies one technology: the rocket. This website will explain the chemistry at the core of the most important technology in the aerospace industry. If you get lost, don't worry; it is rocket science, after all.
